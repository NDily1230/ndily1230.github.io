About Me

  • About
  • Skills

I am Normandily, a certified full-stack web developer that specializes in fast, dynamic websites. Growing up I always loved expressing myself creatively through sketching or painting. Although I have always admired art in any form, I consider myself to actually be more left-brained. I can thank my unusual love for solving math problems and critical-thinking for that. While struggling to determine what I wanted to do in life I came across coding and the rest is history. I found something that fuses both of my passions into one unique experience. I enjoy writing code that delivers the final product in a fast, cost-effective way, while also creating a visually appealing website.
